Understanding Diversity: Tailoring Experiences through Customer Segmentation.

Customer segmentation is the practice of dividing a customer base into distinct groups with shared characteristics to enable more targeted and effective marketing strategies. It helps businesses tailor their approaches to better meet the diverse needs and preferences of different customer segments.

Data Visualization

The application uses a machine learning algorithm known as K-Means Clustering for customer segmentation. K-Means is a popular unsupervised learning algorithm that groups similar data points into clusters based on their features.

The algorithm analyzes customer data, including age, annual income, and spending score, to identify patterns and similarities among customers. It then groups customers into segments based on these features, aiming to create homogeneous groups with similar characteristics within each segment.

Currently, the application is configured to use the K-Means algorithm for customer segmentation. However, if you have specific requirements or preferences, the application is designed to be extensible. You can explore and modify the codebase to integrate different algorithms or customize the existing one based on your needs.

However, we actually recommend using both Flowbite, Flowbite Pro, and even Tailwind UI as there is no technical reason stopping you from using the best of two worlds.
